Some of our products


This enterprise is dedicated to offer society the experience of buying a plant (cacti/succulent), and them being able to see the great experience of seeing a living being grow under your care.










Cacti means "home guardian", this plant is considered to be a symbol of equilibrium and a protective talisman for those that live under the same roof. Their thorns represent security, protection, and persistence. They also purify the environment and are a barrier against electromagnetic radiation. In order to receive these advantages, Feng-Shui followers take special care of where to place them.



The word "succulent" refers to any fleshy plant, whether in leaves, stems or roots, that have been adapted for moisture storage in times of drought. These are distributed throughout the world and can live in different habitats. These plants are so decorative that can be used in many ways to give color and texture to every corner of the room home.

Succulents are very special plants and relatively easy to care for, succulents are suitable for activating the energy of abundance, as they accumulate water throughout your body permanently

According to feng shui they can emit different types of energy and this will depend on the shape of their leaves, its flowers, aromas and its longevity. For feng shui plants store energy by capturing the sun's rays for photosynthesis. This trapped solar energy (energy Yang) is transformed into Ying energy by converting light energy into chemical energy through of the synthesis of organic compounds that supply food to plants.



Irrigation: It is very important to consider the type of substrate for these plants, since many times, having them only with one type, this can absorb much more water and, in turn, not drain it, being detrimental to the seedlings. That is why it is recommended (depending on the number of plants) make a good mix
