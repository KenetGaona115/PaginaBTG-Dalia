About Us

Mission

We are an enterprise that offers our clients the whole experience of taking care of a living being (Suka), offering the needed tools to take care of them, and make them grow with lots of love.

Vision

Be the number 1 enterprise in Jalisco of succulents and cacti, offering the best quality, and with the objective of giving our clients the opportunity of seeing a living being grow strong with support and love.

Values

• Trust: Team Suka is conscious that money produced by the enterprise has to be managed with complete transparency, and any transaction that takes place is reported to the team.

• Quality: Capacitation in our enterprise is very important so we can give our customer a Suka of the best quality. Quality is assured by knowing the special needs of each plant, the correct irrigation method, the correct substratum, and how to correctly transplant a plant.

• Honesty: Honesty inside our team is very important. Any error comitted has to be reported, just like any work completed, so the whole team can know what is taking place in an efficient manner.

• Responsibility: Any work is divided equitatively between our team, so every member can comply with their obligations and offer our clients a product that complies with their expectations.
